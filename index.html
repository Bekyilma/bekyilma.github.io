  • Me in a Nutshell

    I am a Computer Scientist with a background in optimization and applied Machine Learning. I received my PhD from the University of Lorraine (IAEM doctoral school ) in Automatic, Signal and Image processing, Computer engineering. Previously I worked as a Research Scientist at the Luxembourg Institute of Science and Technology (LIST), Research Center for Automatic Control (CRAN) a joint research unit with the French National Scientific Research Center (CNRS), Bruno Kessler Institute (FBK) and University of Trento ( DISI). I was also a Visiting Scholar at the University of Pittsburgh, School of Computing and Information (SCI) .

    I'm AI enthusiast, passionate about applied Machine learning and Data-Driven Solutions. My research focuses on various aspects of Human-Centered Artificial Intelligence, including Recommender Systems, Adaptive User Interfaces, and Personalization in the context of Smart Interactive Environments aka Cyber-Physical-Social Systems (CPSS).
